# Privacy Policy for Connect

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Connect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
All data you create in Connect is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers. This includes:
- Contact names and information
- Phone numbers and email addresses
- Photos you add to contacts
- Connection schedules and reminders
- Notes and preferences

### Permissions We Request

Connect requests the following permissions to provide its core functionality:

#### Camera Permission
- **Purpose:** To allow you to take photos for contact avatars
- **Usage:** Photos are stored locally on your device only. We do not access, transmit, or share these photos.

#### Contacts Permission (READ_CONTACTS)
- **Purpose:** To allow you to import contact information from your device's address book
- **Usage:** Contact information is only accessed when you explicitly choose to import a contact. We do not automatically access or sync your contacts.

#### Phone Permission (CALL_PHONE)
- **Purpose:** To enable making phone calls directly from the app
- **Usage:** Used only when you tap the call button. We do not make calls automatically or access your call history.

#### SMS Permission (SEND_SMS)
- **Purpose:** To enable sending text messages directly from the app
- **Usage:** Used only when you choose to send a message. We do not read your messages or send messages automatically.

#### Notifications Permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)
- **Purpose:** To send you reminders about scheduled connections
- **Usage:** Notifications are only sent for connections you have scheduled. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

#### Alarm Permissions (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, USE_EXACT_ALARM)
- **Purpose:** To schedule precise reminders for your connections
- **Usage:** Used only to trigger notifications at the times you specify. We do not access or modify other alarms on your device.

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms
- We do not transmit any data over the internet
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not share your data with third parties

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- You can view, edit, or delete any contact or connection at any time
- You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings
- All data is stored locally, so you can delete the app to remove all data

## Children's Privacy

Connect is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
